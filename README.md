# What AGI Unlocks

**Live site → [jacobjake1.github.io/agi-unlocks](https://jacobjake1.github.io/agi-unlocks)**

An interactive timeline modeling what AGI and ASI unlock for humanity — from UBI and disease elimination to Mars colonization and post-scarcity economics. Built on the framework that AGI and ASI are effectively one event, separated by days via recursive self-improvement.

---

## The Core Thesis

Most models of AI adoption use historical technology adoption curves. Those curves assume infrastructure rollout delays. AGI has no infrastructure rollout — the intelligence *is* the infrastructure. The moment AGI arrives, it is already deployed everywhere simultaneously. This breaks every economist's timeline model.

**AGI arrives ~2028. ASI follows within days. Everything changes within years, not decades.**

## Outcome Probabilities

| Outcome | Probability | Notes |
|---|---|---|
| Danger Zone Transition | 55% | Gets there eventually but brutal 2028–2033. Most likely single path. |
| Soft Landing | 10% | Trimmed hard — AGI→ASI speed makes managed transition nearly impossible. |
| Power Concentration Lock-in | 8% | Multi-company competition + mass abundance makes sustained monopoly unlikely. |
| Misalignment / Skynet-Adjacent | 12% | Requires self-awareness AND threat perception. Training on human survival data is the backdoor. |
| Extinction | 5% | ASI smart enough to want us gone is probably smart enough to find coexistence more efficient. |

**Key insight:** ~65% of paths reach a good destination eventually. The destination is not the variable — the body count during the transition is.

## Confidence Tags

Every unlock in the timeline is tagged:

- **Likely** — Strong current trajectory. Real scientific basis with clear momentum.
- **Possible** — Plausible but uncertain. Requires things to go reasonably well.
- **Speculative** — Requires breakthroughs we can't guarantee from here.

## Epistemic Honesty

These timelines could be 3 years early or 5 years late. Some unlocks may hit barriers we can't see from here. The sequence may differ. What's genuinely hard to argue with is the *direction* — every mechanism described has a real scientific basis and a current trajectory pointing toward it.

The exact dates are speculation. The destination is not.
